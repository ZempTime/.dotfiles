Our Process

  • 1. Discovery & Planning
  • 2. Design & Development
  • 3. Testing & Quality Assurance

Four-Phase Development Process

  1. Discovery & Planning (2-3 weeks):
    • Requirements gathering sessions with stakeholders
    • Technical feasibility analysis
    • Project timeline and resource allocation
    • Risk assessment and mitigation planning
  2. Design & Development (6-12 weeks):
    • Iterative development in 2-week sprints
    • Regular client check-ins and demos
    • Continuous integration and code review
  3. Testing & QA (2-4 weeks):
    • Automated testing coverage minimum 80%
    • Manual testing for edge cases
    • Performance and security audits
    • User acceptance testing with client
  4. Deployment & Support (Ongoing):
    • Phased rollout strategy to minimize risk
    • 24/7 monitoring and incident response
    • Regular updates and feature enhancements

Key differentiator: Transparent communication throughout. Clients have real-time access to project dashboards.
